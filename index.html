Our Core Services

We deliver high‑precision fiber optic solutions for data centers, telecom operators, and enterprise environments across Europe.

Data Center Fiber Infrastructure

Complete fiber infrastructure deployment for hyperscale and enterprise data centers, including structured cabling, backbone routing, high‑density patching, and end‑to‑end optical link integration following industry standards (TIA/EIA, ISO/IEC).

Ribbon & Single Fiber Splicing

High‑precision fusion splicing for both single fibers and ribbon cables, including mass fusion for 864F, 1728F, and 3456F deployments. We ensure minimal splice loss, full trace documentation, and compliance with data center performance requirements.

OTDR Tier 2 Testing

Full Tier 2 OTDR testing with event analysis, splice and connector loss evaluation, reflectance measurement, fault localization, and detailed reporting. Performed using EXFO/VIAVI equipment for maximum accuracy and certification compliance.

OLTS Power Meter Testing

Tier 1 OLTS testing with precise insertion loss and optical power measurements across all wavelengths. Ideal for validating new installations, verifying link budgets, and ensuring long‑term network reliability in mission‑critical environments.

MPO/MTP Backbone Installation

Deployment of high‑capacity MPO/MTP backbone links, including polarity type A/B/C management, connector inspection, end‑face cleaning, and performance validation. Suitable for high‑density data center environments and scalable optical networks.

Fiber Troubleshooting & Maintenance

Rapid fault detection and restoration using OTDR diagnostics, visual fault locators, connector inspection, and fiber end‑face cleaning. We provide emergency repairs, preventive maintenance, and long‑term reliability optimization.
